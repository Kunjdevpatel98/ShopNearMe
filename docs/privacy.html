Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we use your device's location to show nearby shops.
  • Shop Data: For shopkeepers, we store business details, product information, and order history.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Facilitate connections between customers and local vendors.
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of ShopNearMe except in the following cases:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons.
  • To enable shopkeepers to process your orders.

4. Security

We work hard to protect ShopNearMe and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@shopnearme.com.
